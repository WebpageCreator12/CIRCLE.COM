Circle.Com was founded on a simple belief: that connecting with the world shouldn't mean sacrificing your privacy or security. We are dedicated to pioneering communication technology that puts you in control.

Our Story & Mission

In a world of intrusive data practices, Circle.Com was born from the need for a truly **private digital space**. Our founders, veteran security architects, recognized that convenience and security were often treated as separate goals. We set out to prove they could be one and the same.

Our mission is to **empower individuals and businesses** with seamless, encrypted communication and collaboration tools. We aim to be the most trusted platform for your digital life, ensuring every message, file, and interaction remains yours alone.
